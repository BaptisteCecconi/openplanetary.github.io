  • Community Building

    ... to discuss and implement coordinated online solutions for the mapping community to share knowledge, resources, code, tools and services (e.g. forums, wiki, stackexchange, github, etc.).

  • Use Cases

    ... for planetary scientists to discuss existing and new scientific use cases, both single- and cross-disciplinary (e.g. surface, sub-surface, atmospheres, magnetosphere and plasma).

  • Planetary Mapping

    ... for planetary geoscientists and cartographers to discuss and share tools, techniques and resources, including GIS.

  • Tutorials & Hacks

    ... a place to share and discuss small technical issues, the hacks that might solve them and, if need, request, organise and coordinate longer webinar/tutorials by community experts.

  • Tool Demos

    ... to discuss, and organise online demos and surveys of, existing end developping tools and services used by planetary scientists.
