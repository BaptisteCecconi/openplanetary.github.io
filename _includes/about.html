About

Join the growing community of planetary scientists and mappers
using open source GIS technologies to expand our understanding
of the planets.

  • Community Building

    This channel is for all to discuss and implement coordinated online solutions for the mapping community to share knowledge, resources, code, tools and services (e.g. forums, wiki, stackexchange, github, etc.).

  • Use Cases

    This channel is for planetary scientists to discuss existing and new scientific use cases, both related to single discipline and cross-disciplinary (surface, sub-surface, atmospheres, magnetosphere and plasma).

  • Tool Demos

    This channel is for all to discuss, and organise online demos and surveys of, existing end developping tools and services used by planetary scientists.

  • Tutorials & Hacks

    This channel is a place to share and discuss small technical issues, the hacks that might solve them and, if need, request, organise and coordinate longer webinar/tutorials by community experts.
